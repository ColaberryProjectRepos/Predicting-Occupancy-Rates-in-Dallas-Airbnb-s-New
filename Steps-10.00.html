Importing CSV files into Power BI Desktop 10

Find and connect the dataset folder.

1.     First you need to save the data on your device.

2.     Open Power BI desktop

3.     Click on Get Data ribbon. 

4.     Select Text or CSV format and click Connect.

5.     Choose “FinalFabricFile” file and click Transform Data.

6.     Locate date_x column and unselect all the years before 2014.

7.     Click close and Apply.
